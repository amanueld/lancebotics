We are the Leaside High school robotics team, LanceBotics. Our robotics teams compete in VEX robotics competitions where teams are pitted against each other with the robots they designed and built themselves. The members of LanceBotics work tirelessly to maintain a high standard in the robots they build and to achieve a great level of success in the competitive scene.
